About

I am a first year Ph.D. student at Purdue University working with Professor Ali Bramson exploring the polar spiral troughs on Mars. Previously, I earned by B.S. at University of Maryland, College Park in Astronomy and Physics. There, I worked with Professor Jessica Sunshine and Dr. Lori Feaga exploring the variation of the 3 micron hydration feature across the southern hemisphere of the Moon. For more about my past and present research, check out my Projects page.
