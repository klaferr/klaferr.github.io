Kris L. Laferriere

Planetary Science PhD Candidate


I am a third year Ph.D. candidate at Purdue University working with Professor Ali Bramson. My current work is focused on studying the volatile mass balance on the Moon and Mars. I am broadly interested in combining observations and modeling to explore planetary ices and their variability through time and space across spatial scales.

The pronouns that I use are she/her/hers and they/them/theirs.
